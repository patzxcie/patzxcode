Wind Breaker

Haruka Sakura, an outcast turned skilled fighter, enrolls at Furin High School, known for valuing strength over academics. After a street brawl with future schoolmates, Sakura discovers the school’s true mission—protecting the town of Makochi. Despite struggling with their kindness, Sakura begins fighting for others, not just himself, as he learns the value of acceptance.
